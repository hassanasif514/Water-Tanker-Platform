# Water Tanker Booking and Water Shortage Reporting Platform

## Project Overview

The Water Tanker Booking and Water Shortage Reporting Platform is a full-stack web application designed for residents, societies, apartments, and housing colonies in Islamabad and Rawalpindi. The platform allows users to book verified water tankers, report water shortage issues, track tanker deliveries, manage complaints, and view area-wise water demand insights.

The system includes three main dashboards:

* Customer / Society Manager Dashboard
* Admin / Dispatcher Dashboard
* Vendor / Driver Dashboard

Customers can book tankers, save delivery addresses, place society bulk orders, report shortages, track orders, and confirm delivery. Admins can manage orders, vendors, complaints, shortage reports, pricing, and analytics. Vendors can accept assigned orders, update delivery status, manage availability, and share live location during delivery.

## Why This Project Is Needed

Islamabad and Rawalpindi face frequent water shortages, especially during summer when household and society-level water demand increases. Many residents depend on private tanker vendors, but the current process is mostly informal, unverified, and unreliable. Customers often have to call multiple vendors manually, compare prices without transparency, and rely on uncertain delivery commitments.

According to the project problem context, CDA supplies around 65 million gallons per day (MGD), while the estimated requirement is around 220 MGD. This creates a shortage of approximately 155 MGD, meaning only about 29.5% of the required water demand is being met, while around 70.5% remains unmet.

This demand-supply gap creates the need for a structured digital platform that can improve:

* Vendor verification
* Price transparency
* Tanker order tracking
* Complaint handling
* Shortage reporting
* Area-wise demand visibility
* Society-level bulk ordering
* Data-driven decision-making through analytics and AI

## Main Objective

The main objective of this project is to create a reliable and transparent platform where residents and society managers can book verified water tankers, report shortage issues, and track deliveries, while admins can manage vendors, orders, complaints, and shortage patterns from a centralized dashboard.

The platform will also use analytics and AI-assisted features to support fair price prediction, demand forecasting, shortage pinpointing, and vendor ranking based on real system data.
